About

This site is dedicated to connecting those who write about video games, the games industry, and game development with more readers.

Over the past few years we’ve seen a systemic undervaluing in editorial content. Many of the industries outlets have been shuttered whilst those that remain, only remain in business by creating mass-produced SEO friendly content. I’ve watched as many of my favourite writers have lost their jobs, or have been pushed into non-writing roles just to stay in the industry they love. However, the editorial content is still being made, it’s just not coming from large corporate owned sites. Many of our industries best writers now have blogs, patreons, and substack’s. Some write for free, whilst others need crowd funding to pay the bills. Meanwhile, up-and-coming writers no longer have a pathway into a viable career. These writers are left either penning their own blog on the side just to keep their passion going, or submitting their work to volunteer driven sites.

The problem surrounding all of these amazing writers is, discovery. Alongside the crash of games writing, is also the crash of the traditional social media channels that used to be a reliable driver for discovering great pieces of writing. I made this site so that I can rectify this shift and reconnect the industries best writers with the readers who are interested in this content.

I want those who rely on crowdfunding to be given the opportunity to prove to a new audience why their work is worth the small amount of money that they are asking for. I want more people to be able to read what our greatest writers have to say as they often are key drivers in shaping and improving the culture and education of our industry. And lastly I want those up-and-coming writers to be able to get a chance to show off their work to a bigger audience, hopefully enabling them to grow their writing into whatever direction helps them meet their goals.

This site is my way of giving back to the community of writers whose words have moved me, educated me, and delighted me over the past few years, and hopefully many more years to come.

Whilst I take no responsibility for the content of the work that is linked here, I am a queer gender-diverse person who has zero tolerance for hate speech and content that harms minority groups.

In the scenario I deem an article to be inappropriate for this site and it's readers I will remove it, continued publishing of harmful content may result in the site being removed from this site.

-

I will also periodoically review the sources I am sourcing from for appropriateness and may remove them if I feel they no longer meet the ethos of the site, i.e becoming less editorial.

-

From time to time, especially in the case of a personal blog or a mixed media site, an article may not be thematically appropriate, for example a lot of Gaming sites also write about tech and pop culture.

-

You can make a request to have your site added by filling out the form on the Sources page. Please ensure you read the reules before making a submissions and reach out if you have any questions. I'll attempt to evaluate new requests in a timely manner.

-

Content is loaded in using RSS, changes to RSS feeds may result in articles not being loaded to the site and I will attempt to resolve those issues, but with no promises of a full resolution.

-

Due to the aforemention way of loading information (RSS), changes to the title of an article post loading are not possible, and while I have systematic ways of preventing duplication, it cannot always be avoided.

-

This site is a passion project and so not all requests for new features will be entertained and regular updates are not promised.
